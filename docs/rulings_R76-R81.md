R77 — VAULT OF THE SKY (formation-native rework)
Old (R70): "Move a friendly Unit anywhere on your side; it gains +2
permanently." Measured: R70's permanence did NOT lift it
(−6.9 → −8.0, the 26b ⚠) — the move itself is the low-value half.
NEW TEXT: "Move a friendly Unit anywhere on your side; it and its
new neighbours gain +2 permanently."
Semantics: (a) recipients = the moved unit plus its non-ghost
immediate neighbours at the DESTINATION, evaluated after the move
resolves (both sides where present; edge destination = one
neighbour; lone unit = itself only). (b) Printed "permanently" →
R21 base+power. (c) Move mechanics unchanged: moveUnit primitive,
movePosition channel, R50 picker deferral stands. (d) Composition
on record: the move can complete a line-of-4 — the R63 Bridge
trigger fires via the existing afterAction machinery in the same
action; both effects land.

once-per-round lock, and every other R63 clause unchanged —
constant-only.
