R63 — LIVING BRIDGE (re-text: formation-moment trigger)
Old printed: "Round End: if your Units form one unbroken adjacent
line of 4+, all of them +1 permanently."
Measured failure: 84.8% of games reach a mid-round line-of-4 while
the Bridge fires in only 29.3% of its boarded games — the round-end
survival requirement, not formation, is the bottleneck (Task 22:
unchanged by AI sequencing weight; structural).
NEW TEXT: "When your Units first form one unbroken adjacent line of
4+ this round, all Units in that line gain +1 permanently. Once per
round."
Semantics: (a) Trigger: evaluated after ANY event that adds a unit
to, or reorders, the owner's row — play, move, swap, leap arrival,
revival, token spawn. Fires at the moment the predicate first holds.
(b) If a qualifying line already exists when the Bridge itself is
placed, it fires immediately. (c) Once per round: a round-stamp
lock; growing the line later the same round does not re-fire; the
lock resets at round start. (d) Recipients: every unit in the
qualifying unbroken run at the trigger moment (the whole run, 4 or
more). (e) "+1 permanently" — printed word present → R21 base+power.
(f) Standing rules unchanged: ghosts block adjacency; Rakta tokens
are real units and count. (g) The Bridge LEAVES the canonical
round-end hook. AMENDED ORDER (supersedes the R42/R54 statement):
pre-drain tokens → venomRoundEnd → hook [Savitur → decay → Kamadhenu
→ Sushena/Crucible → Altar → Kalpavriksha → Mahishi] → scoring.
