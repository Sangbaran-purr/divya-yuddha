## THE WAVE'S VISUAL THESIS — "LIGHT IS SOMETHING KEPT"
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
The launch Deva set painted divinity as radiance possessed. The
wave's design theme is THE VIGIL — protection maturing into
retribution — so the wave's light is BANKED, TENDED, PROMISED:
lamps kept through the night, shields as held light, a dawn that
is always coming and never squandered. Where Naga's motif was
venom in motion, Deva's is light in CUSTODY:

- light KEPT through the night → Dawn Sentinel, Vedi Keeper
- light PROMISED, not yet paid → Dawn Banner, Aruna, Ushas
- light INHERITED               → Airavata's Calf
- light REPAID with interest    → Kartikeya, Kartikeya's Vanguard
- light GIVEN to the least      → Kamadhenu, Kalpavriksha
- light SPENT, once, as a sun   → Suryastra (the sole exception)

Use it as a composing tool, not a law (the Naga precedent —
presence portraits win when they win).

## THE WAVE LAWS (every card)
1. SWARGA PALETTE LAW: burnished dusk-gold on white-grey marble.
   DIVINE ≠ BRIGHT — the vigil is held at dusk and in the dark
   hours; keep it dusk gold, never noon. Gold in the art stays
   warmer and dimmer than the frame's gold text (the board law,
   applied to cards).
2. THE SUN NEVER ENTERS FRAME. Dawn is always imminent — horizon
   warmth, pre-dawn glow, light from a sun not yet risen. ONE
   exception: Suryastra, where the sun appears as a WEAPON, and
   even there it burns sunset-red, not noon-white.
3. THE SHIELD TOKEN: Dharma Shield is the faction's one canonical
   light-effect — always the same small gold dome/shimmer, always
   precious, never a wall of light. It recurs on Vigil Rakshak,
   Airavata's Calf, and Vedi Keeper; players should learn to READ
   it across frames.
4. NO halos, NO sun rays, NO script (the board bible's bans).
5. REVERENCE TIER — the highest of any wave: Kartikeya, Garuda,
   Kamadhenu, Dhanvantari, Ushas are worshipped presences with
   canonical iconography. Serene, dignified, gentle handling;
   nothing comic, nothing gory, nothing casual. When in doubt,
   depict LESS.
6. GALLERY SEPARATIONS (hard):
   - Garuda ≠ launch Varuna (the accepted-risk name): NO ocean,
     NO water anywhere in Garuda's frame — he is sky and wind.
   - Dhanvantari classically holds the amrita pot — MINIMIZE or
     omit it (launch Amrita Kalasha owns that object); healing
     herbs + light in the palm instead.
   - Savitur Verse must rhyme-but-differ from the launch Gayatri
     Mantra frame (they are liturgically related; make them
     visually cousins, not twins).
   - Kartikeya's weapon is the VEL (his lance) — never a vajra;
     the vajra belongs to the launch Indra register.
   - FOUR DAWNS RULE: Dawn Sentinel, Ushas, Aruna, and Dawn Banner
     are four DIFFERENT minutes of the same morning — coldest
     pre-dawn dark · first grey-gold glow · racing ahead of
     sunrise · cloth lit by a sun still below the wall. Never let
     two of them read as the same sky.

## RENAME REVIEW — RECOMMENDATION: ZERO RENAMES
The Deva wave does not have Naga's problem. Its Sanskrit anchors
(Kartikeya, Ushas, Aruna, Ribhu, Kamadhenu, Dhanvantari, Savitur,
Ratri, Airavata, Saranyu, Kalpavriksha) are already the register,
and its English names are not placeholders — they are the wave's
second axis speaking: The Vigil and the dawn-line (Dawn Sentinel,
Dawn Banner, Dawn's Rebirth, Vigil Rakshak) are thematic English
by design. Two that were examined and cleared: Deva Sainika is
already a ruled rename (v0.2, collision fix) and is honest
Sanskrit for what the vanilla is; Shakti Spear is mythologically
PRECISE — Shakti is the given name of Kartikeya's lance. Your
taste overrules, per always — but no R84 is recommended.

### 1. GARUDA — Hero · P7 · Legendary
FILE: Devas_Hero_Garuda_P7_rLegendary.png
TXT: ON PLAY: Remove all Venom from your Units; each gains +1
power per token removed.
FUNCTION: The anti-Naga tech hero — strips every venom token off
your side and each cleansed unit grows for what it carried. Hold
him while the poison accumulates; play him as the reversal.
FLAVOR: "The serpents remember his shadow. That is why they look
up."
DIRECTION: the eagle-lord of the sky — colossal gold-white
raptor-deity, wings mantled or mid-descent onto a marble spire,
wind made visible in streaming feathers and torn cloud, dusk-gold
rim light. His myth is the serpent-eater and the amrita-thief:
let the FEAR of him carry it — no serpent appears (gallery
separation from the entire Naga wave), only perhaps a fleeing
shadow implied in the clouds below. HARD LAW: no ocean, no water
(the Varuna separation). Scale is the star; reverence tier.
AVOID: water · any actual serpent · noon sky · halo.
Optional prompt: colossal gold-white eagle deity with wings
mantled descending onto a marble spire at dusk, wind-torn clouds,
dusk-gold rim light, no water, painterly, --ar 2:3.

### 2. KALPAVRIKSHA — Artifact · Mythic
FILE: Devas_Artifact_Kalpavriksha_rMythic.png
TXT: ROUND END: your lowest-power Unit becomes equal to your
highest-power Unit.
FUNCTION: Every round end, your weakest unit is raised to match
your strongest — the equalizer engine that makes every small body
a threat and every wide board a wall. The wave's quiet mythic.
FLAVOR: "Beneath its branches, no one is least."
DIRECTION: the celestial wish-tree on a marble dais at dusk —
vast, ancient, gold-leafed, dim jewels as fruit. The mechanic in
one gesture: a single golden leaf falling, caught in the upturned
hands of the SMALLEST silhouette beneath it, that figure's edges
beginning to glow to match the tall ones beside it. Divine ≠
bright: the tree glows from within like banked coals, never like
a lamp. The vault's centerpiece opposite the Naga Throne — where
the Throne drank, the Tree gives.
AVOID: sparkle/bloom on the jewels · script-like bark carvings
(veto hard) · daylight canopy.
Optional prompt: vast celestial wish-tree with gold leaves and
dim jewel fruit on a marble dais at dusk, one golden leaf falling
into the hands of the smallest silhouette beneath, glowing like
banked coals, painterly, --ar 2:3.

### 3. SURYASTRA — Astra · Legendary
FILE: Devas_Astra_Suryastra_rLegendary.png

it must still lose to the frame gold.
AVOID: noon-white light · a beam/single target · lens flare.
Optional prompt: a burning sunset-red solar disc unleashed low

### 7. KARTIKEYA — Hero · P8 · Legendary
FILE: Devas_Hero_Kartikeya_P8_rLegendary.png
